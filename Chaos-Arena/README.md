Chaos Battle Arena

A small browser game where every click triggers chaos.

You and the enemy draw random character cards.
Some give points, some give advantage, and some completely ruin the round.

The goal is simple: finish the arena with more points than your opponent.

But the randomness makes every match unpredictable.

How it works

Click the Chaos Button

Both players draw a random card

Cards have different power levels:

🟢 Weak Card → small points
🔵 Strong Card → bonus points + confetti
🔴 Villain Card → heavy penalty

The battle continues until the round limit is reached, and the final score decides the winner.
